What awards has Luke Bryan won?
He has been awarded five "Entertainer of the Year" awards by both the Academy of Country Music Awards and the Country Music Association2. He has also won the ACM Album of the Decade Award for "Crash My Party"12. Other awards include six recognitions as a CMT Artist of the Year, NSAI Artist/Songwriter of the Year, seven CMT Music Awards, five Billboard Music Awards, and four American Music Awards1. Bryan was named Billboard's Top Country Artist of the 2010s and the Most Heard Artist of the Decade by Country Aircheck1. He was also the Artist Humanitarian Recipient by the Country Radio Broadcasters in February 2021.
